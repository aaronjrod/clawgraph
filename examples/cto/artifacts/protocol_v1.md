# Clinical Protocol: CG-2026-Alpha (High-Fidelity)

## 1. Study Objective
To evaluate the efficacy and safety of CG-101 (a proprietary interleukin inhibitor) in patients with moderate to severe atopic dermatitis (AD).

## 2. Study Population
- **N=450**: Randomized 1:1:1 (Placebo, Low Dose, High Dose).
- **Inclusion Criteria**: EASI score ≥ 16, IGA score ≥ 3, Body Surface Area (BSA) ≥ 10%.

## 3. Study Endpoints
- **Primary**: Proportion of subjects achieving EASI-75 at Week 16.
- **Secondary**: Change from baseline in Peak Pruritus NRS; IGA 0 (clear) or 1 (almost clear).

## 4. CMC Quality & Impurity Thresholds (ICH Q3A/Q3B)
- **Impurity A (Identified)**: < 0.15% (Qualified threshold).
- **Impurity B (Identified)**: < 0.15%.
- **Total Unknowns**: < 0.50%.
- **Degradation Products**: Must not exceed the qualification threshold of 0.2% or 2mg/day total intake.

## 5. Stability Requirements (ICH Q1A)
- **Long-term**: 25°C ± 2°C / 60% RH ± 5% RH (24 months).
- **Accelerated**: 40°C ± 2°C / 75% RH ± 5% RH (6 months).
