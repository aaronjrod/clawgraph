# Clinical Protocol: CG-2026-Alpha

## 1. Study Objective
To evaluate the efficacy and safety of CG-101 in patients with moderate to severe autoimmune-induced antigravity.

## 2. Methodology
Double-blind, placebo-controlled, multicenter study. N=450.

## 3. Impurity Thresholds
- Impurity A: < 0.05%
- Impurity B: < 0.10%
- Total Unknowns: < 0.50%

## 4. Stability Requirements
Products must remain stable at 25°C / 60% RH for at least 24 months.
