# Investigator's Brochure
**Edition Number:** 4.0
**Section 7:** Summary of Data and Guidance for the Investigator

## 7.1 Nonclinical Pharmacology
In vitro studies demonstrate high-affinity binding to the target receptor (IC50 = 4.2 nM). The molecule exhibits competitive antagonism without notable off-target kinase inhibition up to 100 μM concentrations.

## 7.2 Safety Margin Justification
Based on the No-Observed-Adverse-Effect-Level (NOAEL) in the 28-day mammalian toxicology species, the proposed starting clinical dose of 15 mg daily provides a 45-fold safety margin based on Cmax and a 60-fold margin based on AUC.

## 7.3 Reference Safety Information (RSI)
No new safety signals were identified during the preceding quarter. Investigators must continue routine monitoring of hepatic enzyme panels (ALT/AST) at Weeks 4, 8, and 12.
