# Development Safety Update Report (DSUR)
**Reporting Period:** 01-Jan-2025 to 31-Dec-2025
**Compound:** CG-204

## Executive Summary
This report summarizes the cumulative safety data from 3 ongoing global clinical trials spanning diverse regional footprints. The overall benefit-risk profile remains favorable.

## Serious Adverse Events (SAEs)
During the reporting period, 12 SAEs were reported globally. Independent Data Monitoring Committee (IDMC) review adjudicated 10 to be unrelated to the investigational product. The 2 related events resolved without sequelae following standard dose-reduction protocols.

## Regulatory Status
The IND remains active and in good standing with the FDA. Clinical Trial Applications (CTAs) in the EMEA region received approval without outstanding conditions.
