## Section 5: Warnings and Precautions
**5.1 Hepatic Injury**
Elevations of serum transaminases (ALT, AST) have been observed. Obtain baseline liver function tests prior to initiation. Monitor monthly for the first 3 months.

## Section 14: Clinical Studies
In the 12-week pivotal trial, patients receiving CG-204 demonstrated a statistically significant 15% absolute reduction in annualized relapse rates compared to placebo (p < 0.001).
