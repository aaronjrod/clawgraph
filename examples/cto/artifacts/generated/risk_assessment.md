# Commercial & Regulatory Risk Assessment
**Compound**: CG-204
**Date**: Q2 2026

## Key Liabilities
Analysis of the Phase 2 interim clinical data indicates a mild hepatic safety signal (transaminase elevations >3x ULN in 4% of subjects). 

## Regulatory Impact
This poses a moderate risk of receiving a Black Box Warning in the US, and a near-certain demand for a Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategy (REMS) program.

## Strategic Recommendation
Proactively draft a REMS proposal focused on physician education and mandatory baseline hepatic screening to front-run health authority demands and preserve the first-cycle approval timeline.
