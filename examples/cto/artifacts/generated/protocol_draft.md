# Clinical Protocol: Phase 2 Efficacy Study

## 1. Study Objectives
The primary objective of this Phase 2 study is to evaluate the safety, tolerability, and preliminary efficacy of CG-204 in adult patients presenting with moderate-to-severe relapsing indications. 

## 2. Study Design
This is a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, multi-center trial. Approximately 450 subjects will be enrolled across 15 global sites. Subjects will be randomized in a 2:1 ratio (CG-204 to placebo).

## 3. Inclusion Criteria
- Age 18-65 years inclusive.
- Confirmed diagnosis per baseline scanning.
- History of an inadequate response to at least one standard-of-care therapy.

## 4. Statistical Plan
Power calculations indicate that 450 subjects will provide 90% power to detect a 15% absolute difference in the primary endpoint at Week 12 (alpha=0.05).
