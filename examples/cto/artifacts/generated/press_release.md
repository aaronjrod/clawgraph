# Corporate Press Release Draft

## Headline: FDA Accepts New Drug Application for Novel CG-204 Therapeutics

**SAN FRANCISCO, CA, March 10, 2027** – The Company today announced that the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has accepted its New Drug Application (NDA) for CG-204. The FDA has assigned a Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA) target action date of March 10, 2027.

"This acceptance marks a critical milestone in our mission to bring disease-modifying therapies to patients," said the Chief Medical Officer. "We believe the robust clinical data supporting CG-204 demonstrates its potential as a best-in-class oral formulation."
