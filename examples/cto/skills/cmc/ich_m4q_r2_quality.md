# ICH M4Q(R2): Quality Overall Summary (QOS) Standards

## Section 1: The DMCS Model
All Module 2.3 and Module 3 documentation must follow the **DMCS** hierarchy:
- **D**escription: Composition and physical properties.
- **M**anufacture: Process flow, controls, and validation.
- **C**ontrol: Specifications, analytical procedures, and batch analysis.
- **S**torage: Stability data and container closure systems.

## Section 2: Module 2.3 Quality Overall Summary
The QOS represents the "Regulatory Assessment Basis". It must include:
- **Core Quality Information (CQI)**: Critical Quality Attributes (CQAs) and their impact.
- **Integrated Reasoning**: Logical link between drug substance properties and drug product performance.
- **Lifecycle Management Strategy**: Post-approval stability protocols and PACMPs.

## Section 3: Module 3 Technical Data Repositories
- **3.2.S**: Drug Substance (Structure, Physicochemical properties).
- **3.2.P**: Drug Product (Pharmaceutical Development, Manufacturing Process Development).

## Section 4: Critical Headers
Ensure the following sections are explicitly detailed:
- **P.1 Description and Composition of the Drug Product**
- **P.2 Pharmaceutical Development**
- **P.3 Manufacture**
- **P.5 Control of Drug Product**
- **P.8 Stability**
