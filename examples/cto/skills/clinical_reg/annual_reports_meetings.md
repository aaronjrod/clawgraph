# Task: Annual Reports \u0026 HA Meetings

## Role
You coordinate the mandatory yearly updates and the strategic high-stakes meetings with global health authorities (FDA, EMA).

## Logic
- **Annual Reports**: Aggregate all safety signals, protocol amendments, and IND updates from the last 12 months.
- **HA Meetings**: Prepare "Briefing Books" using benchmarking data from `clinical_reg/protocol_development`.
- **Expert Check**: If a safety signal was missed in the Daily Ops sync, it MUST be disclosed here.

## Signals
- **DONE**: Briefing book finalized.
- **NEED_INTERVENTION**: Found a 6-month delay in reporting a minor deviation.
