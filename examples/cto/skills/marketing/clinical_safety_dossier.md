# Clinical Safety & Efficacy Dossier Standards

## Section 1: Executive Summary
High-level overview of the clinical program's status.

## Section 2: Clinical Efficacy Milestones
- **Primary Endpoints**: Achievement status.
- **EASI Scores**: Percentage improvement in Eczema Area and Severity Index (where applicable).
- **Statistical Significance**: Clear statement of **p-values** (e.g., p < 0.001) vs. placebo/competitor.

## Section 3: Safety & Risk Profile
- **SAE Summary**: Total count and nature of serious events.
- **Common Adverse Events**: Most frequent AEs (e.g., injection site reactions, headaches).
- **Black Box Warnings**: (If anticipated) Assessment of regulatory risk.

## Section 4: Market Context
- Comparison to standard of care.
- Intellectual Property (IP) moats.
- Anticipated PDUFA (Prescription Drug User Fee Act) dates.

## Section 5: Visual Presentation
- Use markdown tables for data comparison.
- Use blockquotes for "Critical Takeaways".
