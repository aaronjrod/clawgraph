# Regulatory & Clinical Standards Research

## 1. FDA Form 356h (Application to Market a New or Abbreviated New Drug or Biologic for Human Use)
- **Latest Version**: July 2023 (Expires 03/31/2026).
- **Key CMC Fields**:
    - **Field 28 (Establishment Information)**: Manufacturing, packaging, and control sites. FEI numbers, DUNS numbers, and specific manufacturing steps.
    - **Field 30 (Cross References)**: Links to related BLAs, INDs, NDAs, PMAs, DMFs, and MFs.
    - **Chemistry Section**: 21 CFR 314.50(d)(1) and 21 CFR 601.2 compliance.

## 2. ICH M4Q(R2) - Quality Overall Summary (QOS)
- **Structure**: Uses the **DMCS model** (Description, Manufacture, Control, Storage).
- **Module 2.3 (QOS)**: The "Regulatory Assessment Basis". Contains CQI (Core Quality Information) and Lifecycle Management.
- **Module 3 (Quality)**: The "Technical Data Repository". Contains protocols, reports, and raw data.
- **Granularity**: Clear separation between high-level summaries (2.3) and supporting technical reports (3).

## 3. Patient Narratives for Serious Adverse Events (SAE) - ICH E3
- **Requirement**: Brief narratives for deaths, SAEs, and significant adverse events.
- **Critical Components**:
    - Patient identifier (Subject ID).
    - Age/Sex/General condition.
    - Relevant medical history & concomitant medications.
    - Study drug administration details (dose, timing).
    - **Event Description**: Nature, intensity, outcome, clinical course, laboratory measures (with ranges).
    - Action taken with study drug.
    - Causality assessment (Investigator vs. Sponsor).
- **Format**: Chronological account of the event's progression.
